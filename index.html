About

More About Me.

A few words about me, I am Quentin, and I am currently working as a researcher on vehicular communications in CARISSMA, Ingolstadt (Germany).

My university curriculum started with a bachelor's in engineering followed by computer systems and networks master's at the University of Liège. My last master's year was a wonderful journey in Lulea in Sweden (where it is dark in winter and always sunny in summer) with the Erasmus program. I am open-minded and like meeting new people. I can speak French (mother tongue), English (fluent), and German (around B1). My Ph.D. topic is sensor data sharing in Vehicle-2-Everything communications. In short, vehicles exchange information about what they perceive with their equipped sensors (cameras, lidars, ..). Because the V2X communication medium is limited in resources (6mb/s!), my research is about smartly selecting which information the vehicles should share.

During my spare time, I enjoy time with my family and friends. I have small projects on the side to develop and discover new frameworks, and simply have fun! You can see below some of my realizations!
